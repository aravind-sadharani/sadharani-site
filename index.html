What is Sadharani?


Sadharani is a belief. That the extra-ordinary cannot exist without the ordinary. That expression cannot exist without reference. That the authenticity of music lies in the transparency of the medium.


Sadharani is a process. To make music accessible. To presume nothing. To provide everything you need to understand and appreciate it. But to add nothing inessential.
